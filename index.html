Binangkal Recipes

By Jimboy Tongao - GP1MA
Ingredients Amount
All-purpose flour500g
Baking powder10g
Salt5g
Sugar100g
Softened Milk50g
Butter50g
Milk120g
Vanilla5ml
Sesame seed100g

Procedure



Dry Ingredients:

In a large bowl, mix flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.


Wet Ingredients:

In another bowl, whisk together milk, softened butter, and vanilla extract.


Make Dough:

Make Dough: Combine the wet and dry ingredients to form a soft dough.


Shape and Coat:

Pinch off small pieces of dough and roll into balls. Roll each ball in sesame seeds until fully coated.


Fry:

Heat oil in a deep fryer or deep skillet to 175°C (350°F). Fry the dough balls in batches until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes.


Drain and Serve:

Use a slotted spoon to remove binangkal from oil and drain on paper towels. Enjoy warm or at room temperature.
